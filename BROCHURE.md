## Core Features

### 1. Smart ID Generation
*   **Instant Creation:** Generate professional student ID cards in seconds.
*   **Secure QR Codes:** Each card features a high-fidelity QR code for instant mobile scanning and verification.
*   **Tamper-Proof:** Designed to ensure student identity is always verifiable.

### 2. Guard Verification App
*   **Mobile Security:** Empower your security staff with a dedicated mobile interface.
*   **Real-Time Checks:** Scan student IDs and visitor logs instantly to verify authorization.
*   **Visitor Management:** Track every entry and exit with precision.

### 3. Priority Fee Split
*   **Intelligent Allocation:** Automatically split parent payments into tuition, meals, or other categories based on your school's priority rules.
*   **Financial Clarity:** Give parents and admins a clear view of financial standing.

### 4. Comprehensive Portals
*   **School Admin:** Complete control over student records, staff management, and analytics.
*   **Parent Portal:** Real-time access to child progress, fee statements, and school communication.
*   **Staff / Guard Portal:** specialized tools for daily operations and security checks.
