Common Questions

Everything you need to know about implementing Shuleni Advantage in your school.

Is student data secure?

Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade 256-bit SSL encryption for all data in transit and at rest. Access is strictly controlled through role-based permissions, ensuring that sensitive data is only visible to authorized personnel.

Can parents access the system?

Yes. The Parent Connect portal allows guardians to securely monitor their child's fee status, view institutional notices, and track progress without having access to other students' records or school-wide administrative tools.

Is the system cloud-based?

Yes. Shuleni Advantage is a cloud-based SaaS platform. This means your data is accessible from any authorized device with an internet connection, and you never have to worry about manual backups or local server maintenance.

Do you provide support and training?

We pride ourselves on our white-glove onboarding experience. Every school transition is guided by our success team, and we provide ongoing technical support and training sessions for staff and administrators.

Can data be exported?

Yes. Primary administrators have the ability to export records (students, staff, financial logs) into professional formats for reporting or external audits. Your data always belongs to your institution.

Is the system customizable?

While the core management logic is standardized for efficiency, we offer customization for school logos, specific fee structures, and ID card templates to ensure the platform feels like a natural extension of your brand.
