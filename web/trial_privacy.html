Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2025

At Shuleni Advantage, we understand that educational data is sensitive and requires the highest level of protection. This policy outlines how we collect, protect, and handle data for schools using our management system.

1. Data We Collect

We collect information necessary for school administration, including:

  • Student Data: Names, admission numbers, grades, and fee payment history.
  • Parent/Guardian Data: Contact details and relative student links.
  • Staff Data: Professional profiles and institutional roles.
  • Visitor Logs: Entry/Exit timestamps and identification details for campus security.

2. Purpose of Data Collection

Data is collected exclusively to:

  • Enable accurate student identification and ID card generation.
  • Facilitate real-time fee monitoring and allocation.
  • Enhance campus security through verified visitor tracking.
  • Provide administrators with necessary institutional analytics.

3. Data Protection & Security

We employ enterprise-grade security measures including:

  • End-to-end 256-bit SSL encryption for all data transfers.
  • Secure cloud hosting with regular automated backups.
  • Strict role-based access control (RBAC) to prevent unauthorized internal viewing.

4. Data Sharing Policy

Shuleni Advantage never sells student or institutional data. We do not share data with third parties except when required by law or necessary for core system functions (e.g., SMS notification gateways).

5. User Rights

Institutions retain full ownership of their data. Administrators can request data exports, updates, or account termination at any time by contacting our support team.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this privacy policy or data security, please reach out to us at privacy@shuleniadvantage.co.ke.
